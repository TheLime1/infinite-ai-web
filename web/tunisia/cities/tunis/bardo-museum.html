Bardo Museum, Tunis

The Bardo National Museum is a museum of Tunis, Tunisia, located in the suburbs of Le Bardo. It is one of the most important museums in North Africa and houses one of the world's largest and finest collections of mosaics.

Collections

  • Roman Mosaics
  • Islamic Art
  • Greek Artifacts

Plan Your Visit

The museum is open daily except Mondays. Check the official website for updated hours and admission fees.
