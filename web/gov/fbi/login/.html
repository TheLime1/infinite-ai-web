FBI Secure Login

This login portal is for authorized personnel only. Unauthorized access is strictly prohibited and may result in severe penalties, including criminal prosecution. Please ensure you have the necessary authorization before proceeding. All activities on this system are logged and monitored.

Accessing the FBI Network

Access to the FBI network is contingent upon successful authentication. This process ensures that only authorized individuals can access sensitive information and resources. The login credentials provided are unique to each user and should be protected accordingly. Sharing your login credentials with others is a violation of security protocols and may lead to disciplinary action.

The security of our network is paramount. We employ multiple layers of security, including encryption, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems, to protect against unauthorized access and cyber threats. Regular security audits are conducted to identify and address potential vulnerabilities. Your cooperation in maintaining a secure environment is greatly appreciated.

Login Credentials

Please enter your assigned username and password in the fields below. Ensure that Caps Lock is off and that you are using the correct keyboard layout. If you have forgotten your password, please contact the IT Help Desk for assistance. Do not attempt to guess your password, as this may lock your account.

Security Notice

You are accessing a U.S. Government information system, which includes (1) this computer, (2) this network, and (3) all devices connected to this network. This system is for official use only and is subject to monitoring and auditing. Unauthorized use of this system is prohibited and may be subject to criminal and civil penalties. By accessing this system, you consent to such monitoring and auditing.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

If you are experiencing difficulties logging in, please ensure the following:

  • Verify that your Caps Lock key is off.
  • Double-check your username and password for typos.
  • Ensure that your account is not locked due to multiple failed login attempts.
  • If you have recently changed your password, allow sufficient time for the changes to propagate across the system.

If you continue to experience issues, please contact the IT Help Desk at extension 555-1212 for assistance. Please be prepared to provide your username and a brief description of the problem you are encountering.
