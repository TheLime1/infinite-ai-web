Unsolved Case #123

Case Name: The Vanishing Violinist

Date Opened: 2023-08-15

Lead Agent: Agent Smith

Case Summary:

Renowned violinist, Amelia Rossi, disappeared from her apartment without a trace. No signs of forced entry. All personal belongings, except her prized Stradivarius violin, were left behind.

Key Evidence:

  • Anonymous note found under the door: "The music has stopped."
  • Partial fingerprint on a wine glass.
  • Witness account of a black sedan leaving the area around the time of the disappearance.

Potential Leads:

  • Check Rossi's financial records for any suspicious activity.
  • Re-interview known associates and rivals in the music industry.
  • Analyze the anonymous note for linguistic patterns.

Status: Unresolved
